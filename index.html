Story

I am the son of two parents who are the youngest in their own families, and to top that off, I am also the youngest in our family. This is a fact I have not shared with many since this fact comes at a cost. Because of this, I never got to meet my grandparents from my father's side and only got to meet my grandmother in my mother's side. I was too young and they were too old for me to meet each other. The most traumatic memorable experience that I have, which you can also count as trauma, is my speech presentation in Grade 11 S.Y. 2023-2024 in Oral Communications Class. Despite its traumatic connotation, this experience has shaped me in ways I never thought I would be. As a matter of fact, that specific trauma was the central topic in my Ateneo Scholarship Essay. My hobby is to play online games. My dream is to become financially independent.
